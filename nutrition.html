Nutrition : A Healthy Balanced Diet

Good nutrition is a critical part of health and development. According to the World Health Organization (WHO), better nutrition is related to improved health at all ages, a lower risk of diseases, and longevity. Individuals may find it difficult or confusing to navigate the basic nutrition information now available, and many sources have differing views. This article offers science-based nutrition tips to help individuals lead a healthier diet & lifestyle.
