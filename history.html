Our History

Since the year 1995, The Hash hut has been known as one of the top brunch restaurants. The brand keeps growing around the world. The The Hash Hut has locations are based in majority of states except NorthAmerica. We welcome guests and take pride in providing the best service in all 50 locations. In 2007, a major deal was set. According to this business deal, Bill Gates of Microsoft and Prince Al-Waleed bin Talal, one of the major shareholders of The Hash hut attained the company in their private ownership. The family of Shaquille O'Neal also retained a significant share in The Hash hut. The Hash Hut has become the icon of luxury dining quality (Guiteras, 2011). Our comanpies dedication to perfecting hospitality and dining experience will never change. The founder of the hash hut was way ahead of time; predicting future demand and supplying it.
